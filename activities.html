JECH The Explorers

Trail Biking

Jump on your bike (or hire!) to try the many adventurous trail paths in Bristol such as Blaise Castle Estate, Ashton Court Estate, Leigh Woods, CLifton Suspension Bridge Circular Trail. Not a fan of trails? Take more relaxed bike rides along the famous Bristol-Bath cycle path or use the well connected city paths to explore!

Climbing Cathedral

With many great climbing facilities in the city, you're never far from a challenge. The Church in St.Werburgh's is a fully-equipped climbing academy inside a stunning chruch building! Great for all ages.

Street Art Tour

Home to the likes of Banksy, Bristol is well-known for it's street art scattered around the city. Full tours are available to help you keen-eyed art appreciators discover these hidden gems, they are ever changing and you may just be lucky enough to stumble upon a fresh peice.

Bristol Sports

Bristol can meet your sports needs, whether you prefer to watch or do some yourself! Ashton Gate Stadium is home of Bristol City FC and the Bristol Bears Rugby Union team. There are many opportunities to do all kinds of sport across the city whether you like the water, bungee jumping or just a relaxing game of golf.
